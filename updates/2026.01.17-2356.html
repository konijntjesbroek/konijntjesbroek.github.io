Big Realizations and a Couple of Steps Back

Overall, I have been focusing getting more consistent in my drawing and daily planning and tracking. It is a struggle and realizing that I am doing about 3 times the work to make sure I am progressing toward my long term goals was pretty frustrating. I am trying to build the system, do the work toward my goals, and log the process. I have been feeling like a failure because I am only hitting about 75% of my daily tasks. After a couple of weekly reviews, I figured out that it was my old friend concrete thinking that was dragging me down. I was forcing myself to do everything on every task despite being in the preliminary stages of growing into the person I want to be.

My takeaway from this weeks review was to classify activities as core and peripheral activities. Current job duties: core, making time for setting up and closing down the day: core, mastering the yoyo: peripheral. Understanding the things that are mandatory in a day enables me to be more understanding of myself and prioritize the daily tasks.
