Welcome to the Arlonian Institute of Useless Information

I have been using this grouping as a catchall for my random ideas that help me to see the world a little differently. Initially, I used it to produce 1st hand research doccuments on papers I created in the mid-90s. Later I moved on to structuring random groupthink and and human thought processing experiments. About this time I started to get involved in the open software and linux movements.

Most of these projects are half finished, but in the process of building out a load of half done projects I learned a good bit about what works for prepping a project. I have saved that knowledge under the layout templates. I hope you enjoy the site and if you feel like I might fit in with something you are working on, drop me a line
