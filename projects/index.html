The Guitars

My longest running project involves repairing and giving away guitars to kids that need music, but might not have the funds to get started. I will pick up just about anything in the 100-150 usd range and give it a go. I also tend to pass around ukes for the kiddos not quite ready for a big stringy thing. What I try to do is:

  1. Make sure it wil hold tune and intonation.
  2. Make sure the frets are in decent shape
  3. Get an understanding of a couple chords and timing
  4. Pass a guitar, a practice amp, cables and a couple sets of strings
  5. Teach the basics of maintaining a guitar
Overall this process takes around 2-3 months, but sets them up with a lifetime of music and communication skills. I also let them know that if they ever have any issues with the guitar to bring it by and we will see what we can do.
